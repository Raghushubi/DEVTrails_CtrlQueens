Frequently asked questions
How is my premium calculated?
Your premium is based on your zone's historical event frequency, a weekly volatility load (higher during forecast events), minus any Gold/Silver trust discounts and no-claim bonuses from clean prior weeks. It updates every Sunday for the next week.
Do I need to file a claim?
No. VeriPay is parametric — payouts trigger automatically when an event threshold is crossed and our system confirms you were active during that window. There are no claim forms, no adjusters, no waiting.
What counts as "active"?
Your app must be open and in the foreground, GPS must match your registered zone, and you must have accepted at least one order in the 2 hours before the event. Your Effort Score captures this automatically.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Coverage renews weekly. You can cancel before your renewal date via the app and you won't be charged for the next week. Partial refunds are not available mid-week.
What if I think a payout was missed?
Tap "Appeal" on any event in your timeline. Our team reviews the signal data within 24 hours and will pay out if the event genuinely qualified. Over 94% of appeals result in payout.
